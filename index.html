FAITHFUL FIVE
A Christian religious nonprofit · Post Falls, Idaho

Faithful Five Inc. is a Christian religious nonprofit carrying the gospel through two ministries: Jesus People Coffee House, our homeless outreach in Spokane, and River Tech School, our K–12 performing arts and technology school in Post Falls.

What we do. Who we serve.

We are a small Idaho nonprofit with a simple shape: feed and walk alongside people on the street, and give kids a school worth showing up to. Both ministries grew from the same conviction — that the gospel is meant to move, to serve, and to build up.

Faithful Five Inc. was incorporated in Idaho on September 2, 2024. Our mailing address and headquarters is 1517 E. 1st Ave, Post Falls, ID 83854. We carry no debt. Every dollar goes to the work.

Two ministries, one mission.

Ministry One

Jesus People Coffee House

A weekly gathering in Spokane for the homeless, the addicted, and the forgotten. Worship, food, clothing, prayer. About a hundred people each week. Several have come off the street and into rehab; some have come back transformed.

Ministry Two

River Tech School

A K–12 Christian school in Post Falls with performing arts and technology at its center. Approximately 75 students, small classes, competency-based learning. Operated by Faithful Five under a concept license from River Tech LLC.

100
People served weekly at the Coffee House

Every week for years, our team has gathered with about a hundred people in downtown Spokane — runaways, drug addicts, the homeless — for worship, food, clothes, and prayer. Through the week, teams go out in street evangelism across the city. We bring people into rehab. We have seen lives remade.

This is the oldest part of our work, and it is still the heartbeat of it.

A calling carried a long way.

Faithful Five did not start in Idaho. It started on a swing in Copenhagen, in a revival in Scandinavia, and in a living room in Tacoma. It arrived in Post Falls by way of a long obedience.
