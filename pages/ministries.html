MINISTRIES
Two works, one mission

Faithful Five carries two active ministries: a homeless outreach in Spokane and a K–12 school in Post Falls. Different faces, different rhythms, same calling.

Jesus People Coffee House.

Every week, our team gathers in downtown Spokane with about a hundred people: runaways, the homeless, the addicted, the forgotten. There is worship. There is food. There are clothes for whoever needs them. There is prayer for whoever will receive it.

Through the week, teams go out in street evangelism across the city. We bring people to rehabilitation centers. Many who come through have gone on to finish rehab, regain employment, find housing, and renew their faith. Helping the homeless has been a red thread through this work since Dan co-founded Trail Youth in Issaquah, Washington, a ministry later featured on Mike Rowe's Returning the Favor.

The pattern

Wherever we have lived, we have started by serving the poor. Issaquah. Seattle. Tacoma. Spokane. The Coffee House is the oldest living expression of this work, and it is still the heartbeat of it.

River Tech School.

River Tech School of Performing Arts & Technology is a K–12 Christian school in Post Falls, Idaho. About seventy-five students. Small classes. Competency-based learning. Music and technology at the center, academics woven through.

The school is operated by Faithful Five Inc. under a concept license from River Tech LLC, which holds the brand and the model. The school is hosted at The Heart Church in Post Falls under a Shared Ministry MOU with The River Church Inc.: a cooperative arrangement between two independent nonprofits.

What the school offers

  • Grades 1–12, full-time and à la carte/homeschool
  • Performing arts (music, theater, dance)
  • Technology, robotics, production
  • Core academics on a Christian framework
  • Small classes, mentorship, competency-based progression
  • Community service. Students serve at the Coffee House

Post-fire recovery

On March 15, 2026, a fire destroyed classrooms at the school's former location. Within two weeks, classes had resumed at a new location. Rebuilding continues. If you would like to help underwrite the recovery, there is a place for your gift.

Who we work with.

Faithful Five does not do this work alone. Two partnerships make the ministries possible, and both are recorded on file.

Partner

The River Church Inc.

Hosts River Tech School at The Heart Church in Post Falls under a Shared Ministry MOU signed July 21, 2025. Two independent 501(c)(3) religious nonprofits, one shared school ministry.

Licensor

River Tech LLC

Holds the River Tech brand and the school model, and licenses them to Faithful Five for use at the school. An Idaho LLC formed by Dan and Marija Hegelund, the concept's originators.
