Stand with the work

The Coffee House runs on gifts. The school is recovering from a fire. Every dollar goes to the work. There is a place here for whatever you want to give.

Give online.

Where your gift goes.

Ministry

Jesus People Coffee House

Food, clothes, and coffee for about a hundred people each week. Van fuel for street outreach. Scholarships toward rehab when someone is ready to go. Every week. Every week of the year.

Recovery

River Tech School fire recovery

The March 2026 fire caused roughly $93,840 in damage. Insurance covered $22,200. The remaining $71,640 gap is being closed through gifts like yours: equipment, instruments, classroom setup, and moving costs at the new location.

Other ways to help.

Volunteer

Serve at the Coffee House.

We welcome volunteers every week, whether for cooking, serving, sorting clothes, worship, or simply sitting and listening. Get in touch.

In-kind

Give goods.

Clothing (all sizes), coats, socks, hygiene items, nonperishable food, musical instruments for the school. Ask what's needed this week.

Pray

Hold us up.

Pray for the people we meet on the street, for our students and teachers, and for the families rebuilding after the fire. This matters more than you know.
